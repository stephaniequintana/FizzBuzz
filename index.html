FizzBuzz will print the number series, 1 thru your number. The list will substitute the word “Fizz” for the numbers in the sequence that are divisible by 3, the word “Buzz” for the numbers in the sequence that are divisible by 5, and the word “FizzBuzz” for the numbers in the sequence that are divisible by both 3 and 5.
